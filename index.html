CODE OF CONDUCT
FST | Standards & Discipline
Every member represents the clan. Act accordingly.
  • 1. Respect Everyone – No bullying, harassment, or hate speech.
  • 2. No Cheating / Exploiting – Zero tolerance = instant ban.
  • 3. Be Active – Log in at least once a week. 30 days inactive = removal.
  • 4. Follow Staff Instructions – Appeals must be private.
  • 5. Represent FST Well – No trolling other clans.
  • 6. No Account Sharing – Alt accounts used to bypass bans are forbidden.
Punishment System
⚠️ Warning → Suspension → Demotion → Ban
