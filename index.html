CODE OF CONDUCT
FST | Standards & Discipline
Every member represents the clan. Act accordingly.
  • 1. Respect Everyone – No bullying, harassment, or hate speech. Be respectful to teammates and opponents.
  • 2. No Cheating / Exploiting – No aimbot, wallhacks, or glitches. Zero tolerance = instant ban.
  • 3. Be Active – Log in at least once a week. Inform staff about inactivity. 30 days inactive = removal.
  • 4. Follow Staff Instructions – Listen to Command & Staff members. Appeals must be private.
  • 5. Represent FST Well – No trolling other clans, no drama. Wear FST uniform during wars.
  • 6. No Account Sharing – Do not share your Roblox account. Alt accounts used to bypass bans are forbidden.
Punishment System
⚠️ First Warning → Verbal warning
⚠️ Second Offense → 3-day suspension
⚠️ Third Offense → Demotion / Rank reset
❌ Severe Offense (cheating, hate speech) → Immediate Ban
FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS
❓ How do I join FST?
Click the "Apply" button in the sidebar and follow the instructions to open our Discord application channel.
❓ What are the requirements to join?
50+ Rivals wins, a working microphone, and loyalty to FST.
❓ How do I rank up?
Be active, participate in clan wars, help recruit new members, and show leadership skills.
❓ I'm going to be inactive. What should I do?
Inform a Director or Overseer. We offer inactive status so you don't get removed.
❓ Can I leave and rejoin later?
Yes, but repeated leaving may result in a longer re-entry cooldown.
❓ What happens if I break a rule?
First warning → 3-day suspension → Demotion → Ban (depending on severity).
❓ How do I report someone?
Contact any Moderator or Director via Discord DM or create a ticket.
❓ Does FST accept content creators?
Yes! We support streamers and YouTubers. Contact Owner for a Creator role.
❓ Where do I find clan announcements?
Check the "News" panel on this wiki or the #announcements channel on Discord.
❓ Who do I contact for help?
Tag @Moderator in Discord or open a support ticket.
