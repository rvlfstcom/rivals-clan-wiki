CODE OF CONDUCT
HH | Standards & Discipline
Every member represents the clan. Act accordingly.
  • 1. Respect Everyone – No bullying, harassment, or hate speech.
  • 2. No Cheating / Exploiting – Zero tolerance = instant ban.
  • 3. Be Active – Log in at least once a week. 30 days inactive = removal.
  • 4. Follow Staff Instructions – Appeals must be private.
  • 5. Represent HH Well – No trolling other clans.
  • 6. No Account Sharing – Alt accounts used to bypass bans are forbidden.
Punishment System: Warning → Suspension → Demotion → Ban

STRIKE SYSTEM
How strikes work:
When a member breaks the Code of Conduct, a staff member issues a strike. Accumulating strikes leads to automatic punishments.
Strike Penalties:
• 1st strike: Verbal warning
• 2nd strike: 24-hour mute
• 3rd strike: Demotion (1 rank down)
• 4th strike: Kick from clan (can reapply after 7 days)
• 5th strike: Permanent ban
Strikes expire after 30 days of good behavior.
